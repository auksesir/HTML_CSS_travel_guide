Travel tips

1. Language

If you want to travel around London without any problems, you should learn some basic English phrases.

2. Currency

Currency in London like in the rest of England is Pound Sterling.

3. Oyster Card

Oyster Card is the cheapest way to travel in London. You can use it for metro, bus and tram services. Please find more information about it here.

4. Planning

Plan your journey and do your bookings in advance.

5. Free Museums

There are museums in London that you can visit for free: British Museum, Natural History Museum, National Gallery, Tate Modern etc.
