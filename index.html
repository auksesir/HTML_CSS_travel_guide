London Travel Guide

London is the capital of England and the United Kingdom. The history of London reaches back to Roman times. London is a captivating city with great architecture and unforgettable tourist attractions. There are plenty of great museums and galleries to visit. Also, London is famous for its restaurants with cuisines from around the world.

Millions of tourists are visiting London each year. Among the most visited places in London are Big Ben, London Eye, The British Museum and Tower of London.
