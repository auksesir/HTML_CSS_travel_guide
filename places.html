Places to visit

Big Ben
Tower

Big Ben is one of the most famous clocks in the world with its iconic bell.

Opening times

  • Mon,Tue,Thu,Fri: 9:15 - 16:30
  • Wed: 13:15 - 16:30

Prices

  • Adult: £15
  • Family: £37
  • Student: £10
  • Child (5-16): £6
  • Under 5: Free

The London
Eye

The London Eye is 135 metres high and takes 30 minutes to complete one rotation. It provides an unique view of every iconic landmark in London.

Opening times

  • Mon-Sun: 11:00 - 18:00

Prices

  • Adult: £37
  • Child: £32

The British Museum

British Museum is dedicated to human history, culture and art. Its collection cosists of items as old as two million years.

Opening times

  • Mon-Sun: 10:00 - 17:00

Prices

  • Free

Tower of London is a castle that was built in the 1070s. It has a rich and complex history.

Opening times

  • Mon-Sun: 10:00 - 19:00

Prices

  • Member: Free
  • Adult (18-64): £25
  • Child (5-15): £12.50
  • Concession: £20
  • Family: £37.50
